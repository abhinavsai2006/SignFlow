# SignFlow - Signature Redesign Report

This report presents specifications for the updated Adobe-style signature block layouts.

## 1. Specifications for Adobe-Style appearance
- **Margins & Spacing**: Margins are optimized to maximize the size of the dynamic text elements within the signature fields.
- **Font Scale & Sizing**: Signer name and email labels use a larger bold weight; the timestamp and cryptographic logs use clean, highly legible sizes to avoid blurriness.
- **Visual indicators**:
  - **Light green background** indicates successful validation status.
  - **Verified Shield icon** in green adds confidence.
  - **✓ Identity Verified**, **✓ Audit Verified**, and **✓ SHA-256 Protected** badges verify cryptographic integrity.
